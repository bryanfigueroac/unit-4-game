Rupee Collector


You will be given a random number at the start of the game

There are four rupees below. By clicking on the rupee, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win by matching the total score to a random number. You lose the game if your total reaches over the random number.

The value of each rupee is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the numbers will be reset on each rupee
